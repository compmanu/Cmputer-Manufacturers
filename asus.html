AsusTek Computer Inc.

AsusTek Computer Inc., commonly known as Asus, is a Taiwanese multinational company that produces computer hardware and consumer electronics. Founded in 1989, Asus started as a modest motherboard manufacturer and has since expanded into a global leader in the tech industry, producing a wide range of products, including laptops, desktops, motherboards, and smartphones.

Asus is known for its innovative and high-quality products, particularly in the gaming sector, where its Republic of Gamers (ROG) brand is a top choice for gamers worldwide. Asus has consistently pushed the boundaries of design and performance, making it a trusted name in the technology industry.
