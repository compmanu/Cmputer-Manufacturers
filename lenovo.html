Lenovo Group Limited

Lenovo is a Chinese multinational technology company with dual headquarters in Beijing, China, and Morrisville, North Carolina, USA. Founded in 1984 as Legend, Lenovo has grown to become one of the largest PC manufacturers in the world. Lenovo is known for its ThinkPad laptops, which were originally an IBM product line before Lenovo acquired IBM's personal computing division in 2005.

In addition to laptops, Lenovo also manufactures desktops, tablets, smartphones, workstations, servers, electronic storage devices, IT management software, and smart TVs. Lenovo's strategic acquisitions and focus on innovation have solidified its position as a leader in global technology markets.
