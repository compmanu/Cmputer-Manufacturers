OnePlus Watch 2: The latest OnePlus Watch 2 has made significant improvements over its predecessor. It boasts a dual operating system setup that allows it to achieve up to 100 hours of battery life in smartwatch mode. This innovative approach combines a low-power BES2700BP chip for basic tasks and the Snapdragon W5 chip for full Wear OS functionality, making it one of the standout smartwatches of the year so far​ (TechRadar)​.
