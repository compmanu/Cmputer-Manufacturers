Acer Inc.

Acer Inc. is a Taiwanese multinational hardware and electronics corporation that specializes in advanced electronics technology. Founded in 1976 by Stan Shih and his partners, Acer began as a distributor of electronic parts before venturing into the production of computers.

Today, Acer is one of the world's top computer manufacturers, known for its reliable and affordable laptops, desktops, and monitors. Acer has a strong presence in the education sector and is committed to providing accessible technology solutions to users worldwide.
