International Business Machines (IBM)

IBM is an American multinational technology and consulting company headquartered in Armonk, New York. Founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR), it was renamed IBM in 1924. IBM is one of the world's largest technology companies, known for its computer hardware, software, and services.

IBM's history is marked by numerous innovations, including the development of the first hard drive, the first relational database, and the IBM PC, which became the standard for personal computers. IBM has evolved into a leader in cloud computing, AI, and quantum computing.
